About Us

Kenya’s leading Web3 technology provider ,blockchain infrastructure and integration hub

Web3Kenya is Kenya’s leading Web3 technology provider ,blockchain infrastructure and integration hub, empowering businesses, creators, and communities to step confidently into the decentralized world. We specialize in smart contract development, NFT launches, token creation, decentralized apps (DApps), and seamless blockchain integrations—giving you the tools to innovate, scale, and thrive in the Web3 era. Our mission is simple: to connect Kenya to the blockchain revolution through secure, scalable, and futuristic digital experiences. We focus on creating technology that works for you—Importantly, we do not hold user funds or provide financial services—we build the infrastructure, technology and integrations that empower you to thrive safely and efficiently, without handling your funds or providing financial services .
